Recently in cooperation with [Daulet Moldabayev](http://www.uib.no/persons/Daulet.Moldabayev) and [Henrik Kalisch](http://folk.uib.no/hka002/) (from the [Department of Mathematics](http://www.uib.no/en/math), [University of Bergen](http://www.uib.no/en)) we performed a study on the relevance of the Whitham equation (which enjoys the *exact linear dispersion relation*) as a model for water waves. Namely, the derivation of this equation from the Hamiltonian formalism was performed and the relevant parameters controlling the accuracy of this approximation were highlighted. Finally, this study is completed by performing comparisons in various regimes with the full Euler equations solved by the dynamic conformal mapping technique.
