title: Dynamic energy cascade in gKdV
---

Recently in collaboration with [Elena Tobisch](http://www.dynamics-approx.jku.at/lena/) (Kartashova) we submitted already our third common work on the observation of the dynamic energy cascades in nonlinear dipersive PDEs. This time our study was devoted to the generalized KdV-type equations (up to *quintic* nonlinearities).

It is known that the classical (quadratic) KdV equation is modulationally stable, while the mKdV (with the cubic nonlinearity) is unstable if the nonlinear term sign coincides with the sign of the dispersive term. Quartic gKdV does not seem to have the Modulational Instability (MI), while the quintic gKdV shows MI for sufficiently large initial data. Our study is devoted to the interplay between MI effect on the dynamic energy cascade formation.

The preprint can be downloaded from the [HAL](http://hal.archives-ouvertes.fr/) server:

* **D. Dutykh**, E. Tobisch. [Formation of the dynamic energy cascades in quartic and quintic generalized KdV equations](http://hal.archives-ouvertes.fr/hal-01070799), Submitted, 2014
