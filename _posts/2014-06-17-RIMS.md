Thanks to the kind invitation of Professor [Tatsuo Iguchi](http://www.math.keio.ac.jp/~iguchi/index-e.html) I am going to visit Japan in July 2014. The scientific programme of this visit includes the following events:

* [RIMS](http://www.kurims.kyoto-u.ac.jp/en/index.html) Workshop: ''*[Mathematical Analysis in Fluid and Gas Dynamics](http://www.math.keio.ac.jp/~iguchi/RIMS/)*'', July 2-4 at Kyoto University, with a talk on variational methods for water wave modeling
* Nonlinear Analysis Seminar at Keio Univerity on July 8, where I am going to speak about shallow water waves with surface tension effects.

Hope to see you soon in Japan!
