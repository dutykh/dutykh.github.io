title: Serre's pseudo-spectral solver
---

I just released a pseudo-spectral solver for the Serre-Green-Naghdi equations on the flat bottom. The scheme used in this Matlab script is described in the following publication:

* **D. Dutykh**, D. Clamond, P. Milewski & D. Mitsotakis. [*Finite volume and pseudo-spectral schemes for the fully nonlinear 1D Serre equations*](http://hal.archives-ouvertes.fr/hal-00587994/), European Journal of Applied Mathematics, **24**(5), 761-787, 2013

The script can be downloaded here:

* [https://github.com/dutykh/SerreGravityWave/](https://github.com/dutykh/SerreGravityWave/)

**Any comments are welcome!**
