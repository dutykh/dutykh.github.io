Tomorrow I am travelling again to the charming city of Bilbao (Bizkaia, Spain) after a kind invitation of [Prof. Elena Akhmatskaya](http://www.bcamath.org/en/people/akhmatskaya/). I am going to spend the whole month of April (and a bit of May) at the [Basque Center for Applied Mathematics](http://www.bcamath.org/en/). Besides numerous scientific collaborations I have with this center, this time I will also deliver a short introductory course on the Hydrodynamics in general, with a particular emphasis on the [Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Smoothed-particle_hydrodynamics) method.
