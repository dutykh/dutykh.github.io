The next two months I am going to spend at the [Institut für Analysis](http://www.jku.at/analysis/content/), [Johannes Kepler Universität Linz](http://www.jku.at/content), Austria after the kind invitation of Prof. [Elena Tobisch](http://www.dynamics-approx.jku.at/lena/). One of the main goals of this visit is to edit a contributed volume entitled *"New Approaches to Nonlinear Waves"*, which will be published by [Springer](http://www.springer.com/) series [Lecture Notes in Physics](http://www.springer.com/series/5304/).
