Tomorrow I departure for the next destination - Valladolid (Spain) for a French-Spanish Workshop on Evolution Problems (FSWEP16). This meeting will be hosted by [imUVa](http://imuva.uva.es/) and [Universidad de Valladolid](http://www.uva.es/). The Programme can be seen [here](http://imuva.uva.es/files/cartelfswep16.pdf). I would like to thank the organizers for giving me an opportunity to speak there. My talk will be devoted to the derivation of Galilean invariant and energy-consistent long wave models.
