title: sine-Gordon equation on graphs

Today we submitted a new preprint (in collaboration with [Jean-Guy Caputo](http://lmi.insa-rouen.fr/membres/10-membres/maitres-de-conference/20-caputo-jean-guy.html/)) devoted to the dynamics of kink solutions of the celebrated [sine-Gordon equation](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sine-Gordon_equation/) (sG). The particularity consists in the fact that the sG equation is posed on networks (*i.e.* graphs), which are not manifolds because of the junctions. This fact makes the formulation much trickier. The preprint can be already consulted at the HAL server:

* **D. Dutykh** & J.-G. Caputo. [Discrete sine-Gordon dynamics on networks](https://hal.archives-ouvertes.fr/hal-01160840/). Submitted, 2015
