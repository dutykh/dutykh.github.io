During the period of 19 - 26th of October 2014 I visited Novosibirsk ("Akademgorodok") and more specifically the [Institute of Computational Technologies](http://www.ict.nsc.ru/ "ICT SB RAS") (ICT SB RAS). During my visit I delivered four talks at different institutes and seminars. Perhaps, it was one of the most intense visits I had recently.
