title: Dynamic energy cascades
---

Recently in collaboration with [Elena Tobisch](http://www.dynamics-approx.jku.at/lena/) (Kartashova) we observed a direct energy cascade in the **modified** Korteweg-de Vries equation (the modification consists in increasing the degree of nonlinearity). This cascade seems to be relatively universal and it should be observed in many nonlinear wave equations exhibiting the [Modulational Instability](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Modulational_instability). The explanation of the physical mechanism along with direct numerical simulations can be found in our first common manuscript:

* **D. Dutykh**, E. Tobisch. [Direct dynamical energy cascade in the modified KdV equation](http://hal.archives-ouvertes.fr/hal-00990724), Submitted, 2014

Later on we were able to observe also the inverse and direct energy cascades simultaneously, which is represented on this image:

* **D. Dutykh**, E. Tobisch. [Observation of the Inverse Energy Cascade in the modified Korteweg-de Vries Equation](http://hal.archives-ouvertes.fr/hal-00991944), Submitted, 2014
