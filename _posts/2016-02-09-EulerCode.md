title: Full Euler solver on general bottoms
---

Today I put on my [repository](https://github.com/dutykh/) at Github the Matlab sources of a pseudo-spectral full Euler solver on general (*smooth*) bottoms in 2D. It is based on the method of dynamic conformal mappings proposed by Ovsyannikov in early 70's. More details can be found at the following URL:

* [https://github.com/dutykh/Euler_bottom/](https://github.com/dutykh/Euler_bottom/)
